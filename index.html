Dr. Muhammad Yunus, a renowned Bangladeshi economist and Nobel Peace Prize laureate, founded Grameen Bank, pioneering the concept of microfinance and microcredit to combat poverty. His innovative approach has empowered millions of marginalized individuals, especially women, to achieve financial independence. A visionary leader, Yunus advocates for social entrepreneurship, sustainable development, and creating a world free of poverty.
